My Process

Research

To begin every project I start by researching and planning. To start, I gather information regarding the strategy of your brand. With the strategy in mind, I start to study the products of your competitors from the perspective of a potential customer and gather ideas about how I should design your product. Finally, I create user journey maps to help visualize all of the ways a customer would use your product.

Design

To begin the design phase I map out the Information Architecture of your product. After that's done, I sketch out a couple of low fidelity wireframes. After you decide which design works best I create a high fidelity, functioning prototype for all device sizes and begin developing.

Development

During this phase I, well, code. It's usually not a linear process where I use one tool then the next. Rather, I go between coding the HTML structure, styling it with CSS, and making it all work with JavaScript step by step as I create each section of each page. For anything on the page that will need to interact with a back-end I use PHP and SQL. These aren't the languages I always use though. My philosophy is to use whatever best solves the problem.

Testing

The testing phase is where I exhaustively check everything to make sure it is all working correctly. That includes anything from dropdown menus and popup modals to contact forms and analytics tracking. This stage is also where I check the site in all common, modern browsers to check everything works and looks good cross browser and cross-os

Maintenance

After launch I continue to analyze the site's user data and make improvement's to the user experience and search ranking.
